# Profile
I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at State Key Laboratory of Integrated Service Networks, Xidian University. Currently, I am focusing on research areas including video compression, video enhancement, and high dynamic range video generation.

## Research and Publications
To date, I have authored over 10 scholarly articles and actively participate as a peer reviewer for top-tier journals and conferences such as T-CVST, T-NNLS, IJCV, AAAI, IJCAI, ACMMM, and so on. My work primarily explores the intersections of image processing and compression in digital media technologies.

## Achievements
My research team, Kingslayer, secured third place in the Perceptual Metric Track at the 5th Workshop and Challenge on Learned Image Compression,¬†Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition 2022.

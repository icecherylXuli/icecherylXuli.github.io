Li Xu (徐莉)

I am a visiting PhD student at the Computer Vision Research Group, University of Amsterdam, under the supervision of Dr. Shaodi You.

Before that, I received the bachelor’s degree from the School of Communication Engineering, Chongqing University, Chongqing, China, in 2018. Since 2018, I have been pursuing the PhD degree in Communications and Information Systems at the State Key Laboratory of ISN, Xidian University, supervised by Prof.Jie Lei and Dr. Gang He, with a strong research interest in image processing and video compression.

I serve as a regular reviewer for multiple highly-impacted journals, including IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, and IEEE Transactions on Broadcasting.
